HopB1a

Results

Three highly-enriched terms were recognized by gProfiler from the D36E::HopB1 vs filtered D36E::ED enriched annotated gene set: GO:0007623, GO:0048511, and GO:0009845, representing circadian rhythm, rhythmic process, and seed germination, respectively. Of the three sets, the intersecting genes of seed germination were found to be entirely down-regulated, whereas the rhythm and rhythmic process terms had a combination of up- and down-regulated inserction genes.
